About

A place for
good things,
well found.

Willify is a marketplace for Kuwait's independent stores — a single, considered place to discover them and shop them. We believe finding something you love should feel like a pleasure, not a search.

Each store keeps its own character: its products, its story, its following. You browse a feed that learns what you're drawn to, follow the names you return to, and gather pieces from many stores into one order. Bilingual in English and Arabic, and built first for the phone in your hand.

It is, simply, the local market — reimagined for how people shop now.

Discovery over search. The right thing finds you, rather than the other way around.

Local, first. Kuwait's own stores, given a place worthy of them.

Privacy Policy

Last updated July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Willify (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application and website (the “Service”). By using Willify, you agree to the practices described here.

Information we collect

  • Account information — your name, phone number, email address, and delivery locations, provided when you create an account.
  • Order and transaction information — details of purchases you make. Payments are processed by our third-party payment provider; we do not store your full card details.
  • Usage information — how you interact with the app, such as products viewed, saved, or added to cart, used to improve your experience.
  • Device information — such as device type and app version, used for support and reliability.

How we use your information

  • To operate the Service, including processing orders and enabling delivery.
  • To personalise your feed and show relevant products and stores.
  • To communicate with you about orders, updates, and support.
  • To improve, secure, and maintain the Service, and to meet legal obligations.

Sharing your information

We share information only as needed to run the Service: with the stores you order from, with delivery and payment providers, and where required by law. We do not sell your personal information.

Data retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service and meet legal requirements. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

Your rights

You may access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information, and object to certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us below.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is entirely secure, but we work to protect your data and limit access to it.

Children

Willify is not directed at anyone under 18, and we do not knowingly collect their information.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time and will post the revised version here with an updated date.
